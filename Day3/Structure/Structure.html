How to Sucess in this Semeter

Start your semester strong by managing your time with an organizational tool like a paper planner, bullet journal, or phone calendar app. Add things like your class times, exams, assignment due dates, and big events. Don't forget to block time for studying, working out, or whatever activities that contribute to your success as a student.That means blocking time for rest as well!

Knowing what you know about your past performance, start your classes off strong by scheduling weekly sessions with a peer tutor. Tutoring is a great way to prepare for and ace exams. Peer Tutors can also help you work through tough homework or assist you in building important study skills like note-taking. Peer tutoring is available for all students, whether you take classes on-campus or online.
